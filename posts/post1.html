Tree Care

Pruning for Health: When and How

By ARBORPRO Team · Nov 10, 2025 · 5 min read

Pruning is a vital part of tree care that helps maintain structure, safety, and aesthetic value. In this guide we cover the right timing, common cuts, and how to avoid common mistakes that can harm a tree long-term.

"Correct pruning protects tree health and reduces long-term maintenance."

When to prune

The best time to prune most trees is during dormancy, but certain species and conditions require different timing. Always evaluate species-specific needs.

How to prune safely

Use sharp, clean tools, make proper branch collar cuts, and never leave large stubs. For removals, consult a certified arborist.

Aftercare

Mulch lightly at the root flare, avoid piling soil against the trunk, and monitor for signs of stress or disease.
