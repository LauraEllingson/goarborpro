Designing Low-Maintenance Landscapes

By ARBORPRO Design · Oct 2, 2025 · 4 min read

For commercial properties, choosing the right plant palette and irrigation strategy reduces maintenance and cost. Consider native plants, drought-tolerant species, and efficient irrigation controllers.

Plant palettes

Group plants by water needs, use groundcovers to reduce weeds, and prefer species with low pruning needs.

Irrigation strategy

Use separate zones for turf and planting beds, schedule during early morning, and consider evapotranspiration-based controllers.
