Irrigation

Smart Irrigation Basics

By ARBORPRO Tech · Sep 15, 2025 · 3 min read

Smart controllers and efficient scheduling can significantly reduce water use on large commercial sites. Pair sensors with thoughtful zoning for the best results.

Controller selection

Choose controllers with weather-based adjustments and remote scheduling when possible.

Maintenance tips

Regularly audit zones for leaks, check sprinkler head performance, and calibrate run times seasonally.
